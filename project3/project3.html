Responsive design is an important element of any project, as it ensures that the content that is being displayed on your site is digestable and pleasing to the eye, regardless of the device that the user is viewing it on. It can have a big impact on the target audience by accounting for who may be accessing your site and where they may be accessing it from, so that user experience does not suffer.

Learn Preprocessing
In order to determine what information your target audience is accessing, and that you are reaching this intended audience, there are a few data sources that prove invaluable. Some examples are provided below.

Audience Overview

This report shows basic information like number of users that visited your site, how many times they may have visited, and what language their browser is using.

Audience Demographics

This report has basic information at a glance such as age and gender, but more detailed information can be accessed such as age groups, interests, and statstics about each of these.

Audience Geography

As a default, this report shows breakdown by country, but you may also view a breakdown by city.
